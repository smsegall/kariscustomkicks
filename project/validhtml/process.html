Process

Each pair of shoes are:

Cleaned and prepped

The shoes are thoroughly prepped from the protective finisher the manufacturer used on the shoe and then cleaned to make sure the shoe is ready to be painted.

Painted

Then using the best leather paints or dye, depending on the shoe material, the shoe is painted with either a brush or airbrush depending on the desired result.

Finisher

Next, the shoes are re-glazed in a finishing coat that protects the paint or dye.

Laces

The last step, is lacing up the new customized shoe using either the original laces or optional new premium laces.

Shipping

The shoes are then shipped out double boxed by USPS Priority Mail, which you should recieve in 2 to 3 days after the process is completed.
