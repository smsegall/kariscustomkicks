Kari's Custom Kicks

Welcome sneakerheads, lovers of dope kicks, or just lovers of wearable art. Kari's Custom Kicks is your plug for one of a kind sneaker designs, restorations, or have your ideas brought to life.

We are available to customize on the materials listed below:

  • Leather
  • Suede
  • Nubuck
  • Canvas
Meaning we can customize multiple brands from Jordan to Vans that use these materials.

Each pair of shoes are:

  1. Thoroughly cleaned and prepped before painting.
  2. Painted using the best leather paints or dyes depending on the shoe material.
  3. Glazed in a finishing coat that protects the paint or dye.
  4. Laced up using the original or optional premium laces.
  5. Shipped out by USPS priority mail.
